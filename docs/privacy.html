Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Effective Date: December 17, 2025

Summary: Breath Bro is a free breathing exercise app. We do not collect personal information, create user accounts, or track you. We only collect minimal anonymous technical data to fix crashes and improve the app.

1. Introduction

Breath Bro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using Breath Bro, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Location data
  • Contact lists, photos, or media files
  • Payment information or biometric data

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

We automatically collect anonymous technical information to improve app stability and fix crashes:

  • Crash Reports: Error logs and stack traces when the app crashes (via Sentry.io)
  • Diagnostic Data: App version, device model, iOS version, and breathing session events to help diagnose errors
  • Session Replay: UI screenshots/video recordings only when errors occur (sampled for approximately 30% of errors) to help debug crashes

Note: All data is anonymous and used solely for technical support and bug fixes. We do not use this data for advertising, tracking, or user profiling.

2.3 Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted:

  • Breathing exercise preferences and settings
  • App settings (sound, haptics, theme preferences)
  • Cached inspirational quotes

This local data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Identifying and fixing bugs that cause app crashes
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Diagnosing technical issues

We do not use your information for advertising, cross-app tracking, sharing with third parties for commercial purposes, or creating user profiles.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Sentry.io

We use Sentry, a third-party error tracking service, to collect crash reports and diagnostic data. Sentry's privacy policy: https://sentry.io/legal/privacy/

Data Location: United States | Data Retention: 90 days

4.2 Zen Quotes API

We fetch inspirational quotes from zenquotes.io. Standard HTTP requests may include your IP address. We do not control zenquotes.io's data collection practices.

4.3 Expo

Our app is built using Expo. Expo may process technical data for app updates. Privacy policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Local data is stored securely on your device
  • We filter potential personal information from error reports before transmission

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Sentry Data: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Local Device Data: Retained until you uninstall the app

To request deletion of data associated with your device, contact us using the information in Section 9. Note: Since we do not collect personal identifiers, we may need additional information to locate your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about what data we have collected
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out by uninstalling the app

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 9.

8. Children's Privacy

Breath Bro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data). To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 9.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and object to processing of your data. Our legal basis for processing data is legitimate interest (app stability and improvement). To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 9.
